How Matching Works

Engine applies insurer-specific rules first, then defaults.

100% Match
Policy No + Customer Name + Premium + Year
All four agree. C.No and Inst No assigned with full confidence.
Partial — High
Policy No + Premium + Year
Strong match. Customer name may differ slightly.
Partial — Mid
Policy No + Endo No + Year · or · EndoNo + Premium + Year
Good match for endorsement rows.
Partial — Low
Policy No + Year · or · Customer Name + Premium + Year
Weaker — needs manual check.
Unmatched
Year mismatch or no combination found
Year mismatch = wrong policy year — rejected even if Policy No matches.
Skipped (Master)
Brok Due within ±5 of zero
Excluded from matching pool entirely.
Inst No / Endorsement Separation

Inst No = 0 (base) only matches statement rows with no endorsement number.
Inst No > 0 (endorsements) only matches statement rows that carry an endorsement number. The two pools never cross-match.
